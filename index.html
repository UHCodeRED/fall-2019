CodeRED: Houston's Premier
Innovation Experience

Hosted by CougarCS, CodeRED is a place for students to have fun, learn something new, explore possibilities, solve problems, and build out their dreams without fear or doubt.

Whether you are building your first website, diving into the world of virtual reality, or building something truly game changing in an industry, come join us at CodeRED Voyage. We'll provide you with everything you need - food, mentors, hardware, swag, etc. Just make sure you come hungry, curious, and eager to build your future.
